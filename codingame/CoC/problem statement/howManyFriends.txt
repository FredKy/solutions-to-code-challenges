Crossing a N-sized square part of Manhattan from the top left (0,0) corner of the map down to the bottom right, you shall only move RIGHT or DOWN.
Your friends live where the lower-case o are.
What is the max amount of friends you can visit on your way?
Input
Line 1: An integer N for the side size of the square map
Next N lines: N chars: The map with the locations of your friends
Output
An integer for the max number of visited friends
Constraints
1 < N < 16
Example
Input
4
o...
....
.oo.
o..o
